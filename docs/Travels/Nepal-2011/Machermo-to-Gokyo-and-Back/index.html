Machermo to Gokyo and Back to Dhole

Machermo is where we first started to experience a sprinkling of snow. It was amazing how a dusting of snow made everything look so much prettier. We also were given a high altitude talk at the medical center in Machermo. The walk up to Gokyo passed some amazing lakes.
